Flying from Addis Ababa to the Rock-Hewn Churches of Lalibela turns a lengthy overland journey into an easy adventure from the capital. Pre-booked ground transport in Addis Ababa and Lalibela helps make the most of your sightseeing time; a single booking saves you the hassle of finding a driver and a tour guide upon arrival at the Lalibela airport, which is a 17-mile (27-km.) drive from the town center.

Check out several of Ethiopia's top spots on this action-packed eight-day tour. Your local guides and driver will take care of all the logistics, getting you from place to place in an air-conditioned vehicle for your comfort. Entrance fees are taken care of, so you won’t need to worry about those. You’ll also be keeping costs down by sharing this tour with a small group.

The town of Lalibela, in northern Ethiopia, is known for its rock-cut churches some of which are almost a millennia old. Spend the day exploring the ancient churches, many of which are still active, and learn about their rich histories from your guide. Get a close look at the rock-hewn structures and even attend a church service in this rural spiritual center.

Explore Lalibela

Experience the Magic of Lalibela! Explore the breathtaking rock-hewn churches, wander through bustling markets, and trek through scenic landscapes. Whether you're a history enthusiast, spiritual pilgrim, or adventure seeker, Lalibela offers something for everyone.
